Welcome to IDS

At IDS, we are committed to empowering the next generation of software developers.Our internship program provides a dynamic learning experience that combines practical skills with real-world projects.

Integrated Digital Systems (IDS) is a software provider company established in 1991. With a team of over a hundred dedicated software engineers, we specialize in delivering full-cycle software development services and innovative software products to clients in Lebanon and worldwide.

Internship Program at IDS

At IDS, we believe in nurturing talent and fostering growth. Our internship program offers aspiring software developers a hands-on learning experience in a real-world software development environment. Here's what the program includes:

  • Guidance from experienced software engineers
  • Opportunity to work on live projects
  • Regular workshops and training sessions
  • Collaboration with cross-functional teams
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Flexible duration to fit your schedule
