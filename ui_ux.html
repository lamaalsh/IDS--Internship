Duration

The internship program spans over 3 months, providing interns with practical experience in UI/UX design and industry-relevant tools and techniques.

Key Learning Areas

  • User-Centered Design
  • Wireframing and Prototyping
  • Visual Design and Typography
  • Interaction Design
  • Usability Testing and Feedback

Prerequisites

Applicants for the UI/UX Design Internship should have a creative mindset, basic knowledge of graphic design tools (e.g., Adobe Illustrator, Sketch), and an interest in creating delightful user experiences.
